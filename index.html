How to Play

Two players each pledge to a faction, then trade turns rolling to build their power and wear down the other's GDP. First to zero the opponent's GDP wins the bout.

◆ THE DICE

Every roll uses four dice: three faction dice and one equip die.

  • Sickle / Hammer = Defense symbols
  • Gun / Plane = Attack symbols
  • Sword = Knight
  • UFO = Alien

◆ YOUR TURN

Tap a die to lock it before your next roll. Tap anywhere else to roll the unlocked dice. You get 3 rolls per turn — after the 3rd roll (or if the equip die shows an X), tap anywhere to end your turn.

◆ BONUS PATTERNS

  • Triple Attack → ATK +1
  • Triple Defense → GDP +3 (capped at 20)
  • 1 Attack + 1 Defense + 1 Knight → 1 Attack Equip
  • 1 Attack + 1 Defense + 1 UFO → 1 Attack or Defense Equip (your choice)
  • 2 Attack + 1 Defense → 1 Attack Equip
  • 2 Defense + 1 Attack → 1 Defense Equip
  • 2 Attack + (Knight or UFO) → 2 Attack Equip
  • 2 Defense + Knight → 1 Attack Equip & 1 Defense Equip
  • 2 Defense + UFO → 2 Defense Equip

◆ THE EQUIP DIE

Its face sets how many points your equip awards are worth this roll: I = 1, II = 2, III = 3, O = blank (no points, but rolling continues), X = your turn ends immediately.

◆ COMBAT

Attack points hit your opponent right away — draining their DEF first, then their GDP. Defense points build a shield that absorbs future attacks. Reduce your opponent's GDP to zero to win.
