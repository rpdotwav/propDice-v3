How to Play

Two players each pledge to a faction, then trade turns rolling to build their power and wear down the other's GDP. First to zero the opponent's GDP wins the bout.

◆ THE DICE

Every roll uses four dice: three faction dice and one equip die.

  • Hammer, Plane, Sword = Attack symbols
  • Sickle, Gun = Defense symbols
  • UFO = your choice, Attack or Defense — you're only asked once, when your hand is finally scored

◆ YOUR TURN

Tap a die to lock it before your next roll. Tap anywhere else to roll the unlocked dice. You get 3 rolls per turn — after the 3rd roll (or if the equip die shows an X), tap anywhere to end your turn. Locking every die and tapping "roll" does nothing — it's treated exactly like choosing not to roll again.

◆ DURING THE TURN

Only two things happen immediately, on any roll:

  • All 3 faction dice show the same face → GDP +3, flat, unaffected by the equip die.
  • The roll claims your own declared faction → +1 pip banked onto this turn's equip value (skipped if the equip die shows X).

◆ SCORING YOUR HAND

Everything else waits until you're done rolling — after your 3rd roll, or the moment you tap Skip. Whatever your 3 faction dice show right then is your final hand:

  • If that hand doesn't claim a faction, nothing scores this turn (beyond any same-face GDP bonus already banked).
  • If it does, every Attack symbol and every Defense symbol in the hand (Sword and a declared UFO included) is multiplied by this turn's equip value — the equip die's final face, plus any pips banked from rolling your own faction earlier in the turn.
  • If the final hand includes a UFO, you're asked to call it Attack or Defense right then, before scoring.

◆ THE EQUIP DIE

Its face sets the multiplier: I = 1, II = 2, III = 3, O = blank (0, but rolling continues), X = your turn ends immediately and nothing scores beyond same-face GDP bonuses already earned.

◆ COMBAT

ATK from your hand is banked, not dealt right away — it all deploys as damage at once when your turn ends, draining your opponent's DEF first and then their GDP. Neither player attacks on their very first turn — any ATK banked that turn carries forward and deploys at the end of your next turn instead. Defense resets to zero at the start of your next turn so it can't wall you off forever — except turns 1 and 2, since turn-1 ATK doesn't deploy until turn 2 and needs turn-1 DEF to still be standing to answer it. Reduce your opponent's GDP to zero to win.
